Steak Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 1 lb skirt steak
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 large bell peppers
  • Fajita seasoning
  • Cooking oil
  • Chicken broth
  • Lime juice
  • Shredded Mexican cheese
  • Flour tortillas, fajita size
  • Corn tortillas
  • Yogurt
  • Salsa

Directions

  1. Season the steak on both sides with fajita seasoning and cut to fit in pan
  2. Cut the onion into half moons and the peppers into strips.
  3. Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add one tablespoon of cooking oil.
  5. Cook the steak, three minutes a side, and reserve to a cutting board.
  6. Cook the vegetables for five minutes.
  7. Reduce heat to low, add 3 tablespoons of chicken broth and 1 tablespoon lime juice, and cook one more minute.
  8. Slice the steak thin and arrange over vegetables.
  9. Serve in tortillas, with cheese, yogurt, and salsa as desired.
