Chili

  • 3 lb ground beef
  • 4 cans kidney beans
  • 2 onions
  • 2 large green peppers
  • 1 head celery
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes with juice
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • Cooking oil
  1. Chop onions, green peppers, and celery
  2. Heat oil in a dutch oven on the stovetop
  3. Add chopped vegetables and cook until lightly browned
  4. Remove chopped vegetables
  5. Brown ground beef
  6. Add back in cooked vegetables, canned tomatoes, and drained and rinsed kidney beans
  7. Add cumin, chili powder, and one kidney bean can full of water
  8. Stir well and bring to a simmer
  9. Simmer, covered, for 90 minutes, stirring occasionally
