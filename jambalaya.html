Jambalaya

  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 2 pounds cooked frozen shrimp, tails off
  • 1 pound andouille sausage
  • 28 ounce can diced tomatoes in juice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 onions
  • 2 green peppers
  • 1 head celery
  • 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tbsp parsley
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  1. Chop all vegetables
  2. Add vegetables to bottom of slow cooker
  3. Chop chicken and slice andouille
  4. Add meats to bottom of slow cooker
  5. Add tomatoes and chicken broth
  6. Add herbs and seasoning
  7. Stir and cook on low for 7 hours
  8. Defrost shrimp, stir in, and cook for one more hour
  9. Serve over rice
